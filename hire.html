Hiring Hall Retainers and hirelings for old school dungeon crawling, a generator by Ed Allen




Characters with a reason to hire on should get a shift in favor of hiring on their reaction rolls.
Stat rolls are straight 3D6, so some classed characters may not meet minima according to particular rules sets. The names of people, places and especially organizations get a bit dark and florid, since I used the namer directly from DunGen where it is designed to name NPCs encountered in a dungeon, more often than not cultists and villains and such. I'll probably make a milder variant that makes fewer torchbearers representing the Blood Drinking Cult of Everlasting Pain* and such.
*I don't think you can quite get that, but it's aimed at that vein.
