Plans for expansion blogged about this project at Ruminations of a Geek

Adding and removing works, though removing nodes does not yet remove them from the list. Save and load are working now, but are incomplete: edited edges and deleted nodes and edges do not save yet. Finishing those up, making the delete button work and adding full node content editing and saving are next on my list. Then probably fixing the canvas to resize with the window. Then expanding treasure generation.
To manually save, copy the text of the room contents table and paste into another file, and right click the map to save the image as a PNG. Print looks best after moving the table using the button.
